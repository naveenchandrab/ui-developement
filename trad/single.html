Divine Discourse

The Sadhana of Love team is reaching out to all Young Adults from around the world to share their Sai experiences and offer them with reverence to our dear Swami. Reflections of Sai is a new initiative that aims to create online spaces in which Sathya Sai devotees can share their Sai experiences to uplift and inspire the SSIO global community. These unique experiences can be with Sathya Sai Baba in His physical or omnipresent form. Devotees can share the way they have connected to Swami and experienced His love for them, and the positive impact it has had on their lives.
